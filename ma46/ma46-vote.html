If you need to look up any of the above terms, consult this helpful wiki article: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/Voting_Terminology To vote another player type /vote [Name]. To unvote type /unvote You can change your vote to another player by typing /vote [Name] again, you do not have to unvote to do that. There will be regular "vote counters" which we, the hosts, will post for you where you can check up on who is currently voting! Voting is one of the town's strongest tools, as it is the only way to reliably rid yourselves from the Mafia, use it, and use it a lot. Not using it means no Mafia member can die. (edited)
