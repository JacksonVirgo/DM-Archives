@Alive Line SP Setup Rules Welcome to Blood on the Clocktower! This is a unique social deduction game based around miss information and chaos. Although getting info is relatively easy, knowing whether or not it is accurate is not guaranteed. This game has plenty of unique mechanics and I highly suggest any one new look through the rules. The Dead Speak! In Blood on the Clocktower, they are not removed from the game. Instead, they are allowed to continue speaking but still lose their abilities, and their voting power is limited. Specifically, a dead player may vote once after they die. Roles do not flip on death, and ‚Äúmajority‚Äù is based on living players, not players that can still vote. Voting Voting in this game is nomination based and is slightly modified for streamlining purposes. Nominations can be made starting halfway through the day(24h). Each alive player may publicly nominate 1 player per day; once a nomination has been made, all players have 12h to privately vote ‚Äúyes‚Äù or ‚Äúno‚Äù on whether that player should be executed. The day may be extended by up to 12h if a nomination is made that would extend the day beyond this time; the nomination is ignored. Votes are revealed once the nomination time is over. Normal play continues while one or more nominations are happening; at the end of the day, whichever nomination has the most votes is executed. If there is a tie, then there is no execution. What does ‚ÄúMight‚Äù mean exactly? Might means‚Ä¶ well, might. More specifically, it means that the host chooses. Yep, that's right, this game has host manipulation built into it; if you don‚Äôt like that, tots understand, but you should probably skip this one. So why is this a feature of the game? There are two main reasons for this. First, as stated before, this game focuses on uncertainty, and this adds to it. Second, it allows the host to help the game stay interesting; if one side is steamrolling, the host can potentially make the game more interesting by one of these decisions. Demons and their Minions The evils in this game share no night chat and have only limited knowledge of each other. All minions know who the demon is, and the Demon knows who all minions are. Due to their lack of night chat, evils can only talk in secret by whispering. Whispering All players may Whisper 3 times per day. To do so, @ me in your private chat with the massage and target player. All whispers are announced, and any player may respond to a whisper for free. (The original whisperer can not respond for free to the free whisper.) The demon has one Demon whisper. They may use this to send a message to all Minions secretly. Winning! Good(Townsfolk & Outsiders) wins if the Demon is dead. Evil(Minions & Demon) wins if only 2 players remain and the Demon is alive. Misc. stuff Only one execution can happen in a day; if an effect causes an excision before the day would normally end, the day ends early. A player that is poisoned has all their actions fail. Any info they gain is randomized. Also make sure you are in this server if you are not already https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
