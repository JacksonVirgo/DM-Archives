@Alive Line SP I would like to remind those of you who are not aware of it of the recent protection and immunity feedback overhaul.

Protecting roles

  • When protecting someone, the [role]'s target learns of being protected by someone else when protecting from an attack. This does not hint towards the role of the protector.
  • Players protected by the [role] will only learn of being protected once per attack, even if other players also protected the [role]'s target.
  • Protection looks identical to night immunity for attackers.
  • Protection takes priority over any night immunity.

Killers (Town, Coven, Neutral)

  • If the [role]'s target was protected or had night immunity, then the [role] will learn that the attack failed, but not what caused it to fail.

Mafia

  • When using the factional kill on a protected or night immune target, the Mafia member learns that the attack failed, but not what caused it to fail.

Night Immune roles

  • Anyone attacking the [role] will learn that the attack failed, but not why.
  • Protection takes priority over the [role]'s night immunity, the [role] will learn of any successful protections from attacks targeting it.
