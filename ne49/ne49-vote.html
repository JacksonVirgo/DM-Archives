Voting Explanation @Alive Line NE This will explain how voting works and how you vote other players.
Plurality voting: Enabled Majority voting: Enabled starting Day 2. NoLynch: Disabled
If you need to look up any of the above terms, consult this helpful wiki article: https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/Voting_Terminology
To vote another player type /vote [Name]. To unvote type /unvote can change your vote to another player by typing /vote [Name] again, you do not have to unvote to do that.
There will be regular "vote counters" which I will post for you where you can check up on who is currently voting! Feel free to ask for one at any time as well! If you vote for someone not in the game, or nonsense votes, they will be counted as self votes. (edited)
