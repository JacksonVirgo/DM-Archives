~ ~ ~ Welcome to NE59: Check On Your Neighbor! ~ ~ ~

This is an open 13P setup with 8 citizens, 2 town power roles, and 3 mafias (as opposed to the usual team of 2). This game will also feature some not very regular roles in this queue: Town Neighbourizer and Mafia Neapolitan! The ability of this town PR is to each night add someone to a private server, creating a "neighborhood" of their own, while the mafia role allows them to check a player every night and determine if they have special abilities or not.

Setup

+ 8 Citizens + 1 Cop + 1 Neighbourizer - 2 Goons - 1 Neapolitan

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Always Enabled Majority: Enabled D2+ NoLynch: Enabled D2+ Ties: RNG if No Lynch is not involved (No Lynch takes priority). No Votes: No Lynch

Game Mechanics

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Mafia has a 24/7 private chat and can multitask. The neighborhood is a private 24/7 chat.

Activity Requirements

Prods: At least 20 posts each 24 hours. - These 20 posts must be in relation to the game. - 0 posts in 24h will result in 2 prods. - If you get 3 prods, you will be replaced. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1h+ each day).

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.

Additional Servers

Please join the following server, it is required for this game.
  • https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
Please ensure you are able to potentially join another additional server, such as mafia chat or neighborhood chat.
