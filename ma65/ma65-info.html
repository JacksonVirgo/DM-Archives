~ ~ ~ Welcome to MA65: Knight-Errant! ~ ~ ~

This is an 18P setup with 13 towns, 4 mafias, and a serial killer. In this setup, 2 power roles and 2 mafia roles, as well as the abilities of the serial killer will be determined by randomly selecting a knight move in chess on a given 4x4 table!

Setup

+ 9 Citizens + 2 Masons + 2 Random Towns - 2 Goons - 2 Random Mafias = 1 Jack-of-all-trades Serial Killer To learn more about the setup (including remaining possible town and mafia roles): https://wiki.mafiascum.net/index.php?title=Knight-Errant

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled Majority: Enabled (with a 24h cooldown in Day 1) NoLynch: Enabled D2+ Ties: First to reach if No Lynch is not involved (No Lynch takes priority). No Votes: RNG

Game Mechanics

Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Mafia has a 24/7 private chat and can't multitask. Feedback: None except investigative results. Kill credit: None

Activity Requirements

Prods: At least 25 posts each 24 hours. - These 25 posts must be in relation to the game. - 0 posts in 24h will result in 2 prods. - If you get 3 prods, you will be replaced. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1h+ each day).

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.
