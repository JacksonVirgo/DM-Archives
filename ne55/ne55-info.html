Welcome to NE55: NewD3

Newcomer Game 55 brings us back to NewD3, your favourite Matrix-like! It features 9 possible setups, one of which randomly chosen off of a matrix. An image will be attached at the bottom, but you can also find the setup here

Phase Mechanics

Day Phases: 48 Hours Night Phases: 24 Hours

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled Majority: Enabled starting day 2 Skipping: Enabled starting day 2 Abstaining: Results in an RNG elimination (no votes placed by anyone) Plurality Ties: Results in an RNG elimination Votelock: Enabled during lylo/mylo
  • If you need to look up any of the above terms go here

Mafia Mechanics

Evil Chat: The mafia has a private server to communicate in secret. Day Talk: The mafia chat is open during all phases. Multitasking: A mafia member with a special ability may also perform the faction kill at the same time.

Activity Requirements

Post Requirement: 20 pots / 24 hours (So 40 posts per full day phase of 48 hours) Warnings: 2 warnings. Third warning = you are removed from the game and punished for inactivity.

Other Stuff

  • Please read the game rules at #📜rules
  • Please join this server: https://discord.gg/4ygmH7b
    • Please make sure to be able to join the Mafia Server in case you roll mafia.
  • You will receive a Mentor to help you out, unless you specifically ask not to receive one (as long as there are enough Mentors signing up)
  • Joining the signup is open for everyone with less than 6 games played on this server.
    • If you played more than 5 games, you may join after 24 hours have passed since the signup went live.
