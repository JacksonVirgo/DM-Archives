~ ~ ~ Welcome to NE66: Whispering Pines Camping - 2d3! ~ ~ ~

Welcome to Whispering Pines Camping, where nature's embrace welcomes you to a world of adventure and discovery. Tucked away in the heart of the forest, our camp beckons with the promise of serenity and exploration. We hope you will have a wonderful stay with us, your hosts, @Xiaysa and @Ngekop! The following will be the rules, mechanics as well as the setup of this game:

Phases

Starting Phase: Daystart Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours

Setup

- 5 Citizens - 1 Mafia Goon - The remaining 3 roles will be determined by picking randomly a number in 1/2/3 and a letter in A/B/C that will determine the row and column of the setup that will be played To learn more about the setup click the following (if you have any doubts you can also dm me or ask in the thread): https://discord-mafia-role-cards.fandom.com/wiki/2d3.

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled starting day 2. - If a player is voted by more than half of the currently living players then the day immediately ends with that playerâ€™s death. NoLynch: Enabled starting day 2 - If this option has the most votes at the end of the day then nobody dies. If this option is voted by over half the players then the day immedaitely ends with nobody dying. Ties: Resolved by RNG (except if No Lynch) - If two or more players are tied in most votes when the day ends, a random one is chosen to be eliminated. If one of the tied votes is in the No Lynch option with more than 3 players alive, however, it will take priority and no one will get lynched. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is eliminated.

Private Chat and Mafia Mechanics

Mafia has a 24/7 private chat. - The Mafia will have their own private server. The Mafia has access to multitasking. - The Mafia may use abilities AND kill.

Activity Requirements

Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 20 times each 24 hours of a day phase or you will get prodded. If you get 3 prods, you will be forced replaced. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 20 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. - If you get prodded by making 0 posts in a 24h period, you will receive 2 prods instead. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1 hour or more each day).

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our https://discord.com/channels/648663810772697089/649058728498429983 channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them. Breaking our rules can lead to punishments, such as game bans, or forceful replacement by a different player, which I would like to avoid if possible. I will be strict when it comes to toxicity so please do keep that in mind and treat each other with respect~
