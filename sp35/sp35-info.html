  • There can only be 2 Town Power roles rolled.
  • There can only be 1 Mafia Head role rolled.
  • There can only be 4 Mafia roles total rolled, not counting Mafia Espionage.
  • There can only be 1 Mafia Espionage role rolled.
  • There can only be 1 Coven Head role rolled.
  • There can only be 4 Coven roles total rolled.
  • If a role specifies that it is unique, that role can only be rolled once and can't be duplicated. ### Phases
The game starts with Day 1. Day phases are 48 hours. Night phases are 24 hours.

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Enabled - The player with the most votes after 48 hours will die. Majority: Enabled starting day 2. - If a player is voted by half of the currently living players +1 then the day immediately ends with that players death. NoLynch: Enabled starting day 2 - If this option has the most votes at the end of the day then nobody dies. If this option is voted by half of the living players +1, then the day immedaitely ends with nobody dying. Ties: Resolved by RNG - If two or more players are tied in votes when the day ends, a random one is chosen to be eliminated. No Votes: Resolved by RNG - If nobody is voted at the end of the day, a random player is eliminated. Amount Required for Majority: Unknown - GIM is weird. There will be some circumstances where players appear alive or dead when they actually aren't, or there's another circumstance that changes what majority actually is. Therefore, no one will know the amount of votes required to kill someone with a majority vote.

Factional Evil Mechanics

  • The Mafia and Coven each have a separate 24/7 private chat where they may communicate with each other.
  • The Mafia cannot multitask, meaning having one perform the factional kill and their own ability at the same time.
  • On Night 3, the Coven gain access to the Necronomicon, which first goes to the Coven Head if itâ€™s alive, and if/when the Coven Head is dead or not in the game, it randomly goes to a Coven Evil member unless otherwise specified.

Activity Requirements

Minimum post count: You are required to post at least 25 times each 24 hours of a day phase. - Mod discretion may result in variation however. Minimum content posts: You are required to post these 25 messages in relation to the game. - Posts that are not related to the game would be off-topic posts, or posts that don't contribute to the game in a meaningful way, such as small talk for example. Replacements: Each slot may only see two replacements total, after which it will instead be modkilled if another replacement becomes necessary. Prods: 3 Strike System - If you receive 3 prods, you will be forcefully replaced. - Prods will be delivered in playerchats, as some abilities prevent players from speaking during the day. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1 hour or more each day).
