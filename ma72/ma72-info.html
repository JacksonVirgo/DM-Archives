~ ~ ~ Welcome to MA72: Pick your Power X/Y! ~ ~ ~

Pick your Power X/Y is a popular 14 player forum setup where you choose which role you want to be. There will be an additional pregame phase at the start of which you will receive your alignment (Town/Mafia) and during which you submit which role you want to be alongside a number from 1 to 100. Roles will be handed out using a draft system, the order of the draft is determined by the number you submitted. It is possible to not get the role you wanted if someone else got it before you in the draft, so choose your role and number wisely! Read about the possible roles and the draft system here

Setup - 14P

+ Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen + Citizen - Goon - Goon - Goon

Draft Mechanics

  • Vanilla roles are handed out, Mafia joins their faction chat and may communicate for all future steps.
  • Every player submits a number from 1 to 100 and a role of their choice from the 8 buckets (each has 2 roles).
  • The draft order is determined by who submitted the lowest unique number, numbers being submitted multiple times will be placed lowest in the draft order.
Example 1. 1 2. 2 3. 10 4. 15 5. 6, 6 6. 9, 9 7. 4, 4, 4 As you can see, "4" is at the bottom, even though it is a low number, due to being submitted 3 times. 6 and 9 are above it since they were only submitted 2 times and are in order again but are still lower than the unique 15.
  • Roles are handed out according to the draft order. If a role bucket had a role handed out no more roles of that bucket will be handed out.
    • It is possible for there to be up to 8 PRs (one of each bucket), but there can also be just 1 if everyone chose the same bucket. (This is unlikely since the mafia will coordinate to pick from 3 of them)
  • The draft is not revealed but may be discussed at any time at the risk of revealing who the PRs are.

Buckets

  • Night 3 Vigilante or Vengeful
  • 1-Shot Vigilante or 1-Shot PGO
  • Cop or 1-Shot Redirector
  • 1-Shot Commuter or 1-Shot Watcher
  • Doctor or Roleblocker
  • Universal Backup or Rolecop
  • Neighbourizer or Fruit Vendor
  • Jailkeeper or Tracker

Phase Mechanics

Pregame: 24 Hours Day: 48 Hours Night: 24 Hours Day Start Time: 21UTC+0 Starting Phase: Day 1

Factional Mechanics

Day talk: Enabled (aka, mafia can chat privately at all times) Multitasking: Enabled (aka, a mafia PR may use the factional kill alongside its role action)

Voting Mechanics

Plurality: Always Enabled Majority: Enabled D2+ Skipping: Enabled D2+ Abstaining: Random Elimination among all living players. Ties: Random Elimination among the tied players. Votelock: Votes locked lylo/mylo.

Feedback and Game Mechanics

Flips: Roles flip upon all deaths. Kill Feedback: None, killers are not known. Night Feedback: None, unless an investigative ability was used. Charges: Ability charges are ALWAYS consumed, even if the action did not succeed.

Activity Requirements

Prods: At least 25 posts each 24 hours. Failure to meet this requirement will result in a "Prod". - These 25 posts must be in relation to the game. - If you get 3 prods, you will be replaced. Please only sign up for this game if you are able to commit an adequate amount of time to it (1h+ each day). Being replaced will result in signup bans.

Rules

Please make sure to have read the Game Rules section of our #📜rules channel, so that you do not accidentally break one of them, since it can lead to punishments, such as game bans or forceful replacement, which I would like to avoid if possible.
